title: "Competing with Intel and Nvidia: The Revival of Advanced Micro Devices (AMD)" 
date: 2021-06-22
author: ["Bernardo Sousa","Cláudia Alves","Marta Mendes","Manuel Au-Yong-Oliveira"]
description: "This paper reviews Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) - a company that was struggling with adaptation issues and potential bankruptcy, restructured its strategy under Dr. Lisa Su’s leadership. By focusing on high-performance computer components, AMD regained its position as a market leader. The study relied on interviews, the company’s website, and academic articles to understand the strategies that influenced AMD’s resurgence."
